# AI Log

This directory is for curated AI collaboration notes, not raw transcripts.

Use these templates to summarize how AI assistance was used, what was accepted or rejected, and how the final change was validated. Keep entries concise and suitable for review.

Do not store:

- secrets
- credentials
- private prompts
- proprietary content
- sensitive data
- raw chat transcripts

AI logs may be kept private, summarized before sharing, or omitted when they are not useful for the project.
